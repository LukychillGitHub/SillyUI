Silly UI

Interfaze Silly

SILLY UI is a playful and experimental interface designed to mix humor, chaos, and interaction. Inspired by April Fools vibes and bad UX memes, this project includes a variety of ridiculous and over-the-top features, such as:

  • 🎤 Screaming Buttons: Buttons that scream when clicked. Because... why not?
  • 🐱 Cursor-following Cat: A cat that chases your mouse.
  • 💥 Exploding Buttons: Buttons that inflate and burst with a sound.
  • 🔐 BAD Login UI (v2): Every keypress triggers a confirmation modal, and the system gets increasingly annoyed if you refuse. Seriously.
  • 🌍 Language Roulette: Spin through flags to “select” your language.
  • 🤖 Auto-typing Reviews: Fake reviews appear as if typed live by a bot.
  • 🌚 Dark Mode Surprise: You really, really should try dark mode. Just do it.

This project isn’t meant to be practical — it’s a parody of frustrating design choices and over-engineered interactions. It exists to make you laugh, roll your eyes, or both.
